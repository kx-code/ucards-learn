## Canadian Cryptocurrency Tax Tips

Canada has clear tax rules for cryptocurrency:

- **Capital Gains Tax** — Profits from selling cryptocurrency are subject to Capital Gains Tax (50% of gains are included in taxable income)
- **Goods and Services Tax (GST)** — GST still applies when purchasing goods and services with cryptocurrency
- **Holding is not taxed** — Simply holding cryptocurrency does not trigger tax
- **Annual exemption** — 2025 capital gains exemption of CAD 16,275 (within the basic personal amount)
