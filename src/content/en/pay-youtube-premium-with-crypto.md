## YouTube Premium + Crypto: Common Questions

**Does YouTube accept cryptocurrency?**
No. YouTube (Google) accepts credit/debit cards, PayPal, and some carrier billing. A virtual card converts your crypto into a Visa payment that Google processes as a standard card transaction.

**Can I share YouTube Premium with my family using a virtual card?**
Yes. The Family plan ($22.99/month) supports up to 6 family members. When you subscribe with your virtual card, you can invite family members through Google Family Group.

**Will YouTube know I am paying with crypto?**
No. YouTube sees a standard Visa card transaction. The cryptocurrency funding is completely invisible to Google.

**What if I cancel YouTube Premium?**
Your Premium benefits continue until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account reverts to free YouTube with ads. Any remaining balance on your uCards card stays available for other subscriptions or purchases.

**Can I pay for YouTube Premium annually?**
YouTube does not currently offer annual billing for Premium in most regions. Monthly payments through your virtual card are the standard option.
