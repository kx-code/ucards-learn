## Why Colombians Need Virtual Credit Cards

Colombia's digital economy is thriving. Over 35 million Colombians use the internet regularly, and e-commerce grew 25% in 2025 to surpass $18 billion in annual sales. Yet despite this growth, international online payments remain a challenge for many Colombians.

Colombian-issued debit cards — even those from major banks like Bancolombia, Davivienda, and BBVA Colombia — face issues when used on international platforms. Many cards are blocked for cross-border transactions by default, requiring customers to visit branches to activate international spending. Paying for SaaS tools, running ad campaigns, subscribing to professional services, and shopping on international sites all require a reliable international payment method.

Crypto-funded virtual cards provide a practical, fast, and cost-effective solution.
