## In-Depth Comparison by Core Dimensions

### 1. KYC Requirements and Privacy Protection

This is the biggest difference between the two platforms.

**uCards:**

- No KYC verification required at all
- No ID, passport, or proof of address needed
- No bank account required
- Uses Web3Auth decentralized identity authentication
- Platform does not hold sensitive personal data
- Registration requires only a crypto wallet or Google account

**RedotPay:**

- Requires government-issued ID submission
- Requires selfie verification (liveness detection)

- User data stored on centralized servers
- Phone number required

**Winner: uCards** — If you value privacy and don't want to hand your ID documents to a third-party platform, uCards is the only choice.
