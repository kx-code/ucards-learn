## Choosing the Best Network for SaaS Payments

### For Monthly Subscriptions Under $100

**TRON (TRC-20)** is recommended. The ~$0.50 transfer fee is negligible for monthly top-ups. Confirmations arrive in 1-2 minutes.

### For High-Value Annual Subscriptions

**BSC (BEP-20)** offers the lowest fees (~$0.05) and fastest confirmations. Ideal when paying for annual plans ($100+).

### For One-Time Large Purchases

Any network works. For a single large payment, the fee difference between networks is small relative to the transaction amount.

## FAQ

### What happens if my card has insufficient balance when a subscription renews?

The payment will fail, and the SaaS provider will typically retry after 1-3 days and send you an email notification. Most services do not immediately cancel your account for a single failed payment.

### Can I switch from a credit card to a virtual card on existing subscriptions?

Yes. Go to the billing settings of any SaaS tool and update the payment method with your virtual card number. The change takes effect immediately.

### Do virtual cards work for free trials that require a card on file?

Yes. Virtual cards work for free trials. To avoid unexpected charges after the trial ends, use a card with limited balance or cancel before the trial expires.

### Can I pay for AWS or Google Cloud with a virtual card?

Yes. Both AWS and Google Cloud accept virtual Visa/Mastercard for billing. Be aware that cloud spending can be variable, so ensure your card balance covers expected usage.
