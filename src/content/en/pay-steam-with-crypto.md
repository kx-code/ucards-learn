## Why Buy Steam Games with Cryptocurrency?

Steam is the world's largest PC gaming platform, with over 30,000 games and 132 million active users. Whether you are picking up the latest AAA release at $59.99 or grabbing indie titles during a Steam Sale for a few dollars, you need a payment method Steam accepts.

Not everyone has a credit card. Some gamers live in countries where international card payments are restricted. Others prefer not to link their personal bank details to gaming platforms. Using a virtual card funded with cryptocurrency solves all of these issues.

### Two ways to buy on Steam with crypto

1. **Direct purchase** — Use your virtual card to buy games directly, just like a regular Visa card
2. **Steam Wallet top-up** — Add funds to your Steam Wallet using the virtual card, then buy games with wallet balance

Both methods work. Direct purchase is simpler for single game purchases. Wallet top-up is useful during sales when you want to grab multiple games quickly.

## How to Buy Steam Games with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Get your uCards virtual card

Visit [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk) and create an account. No KYC verification, no document uploads. Your virtual Visa card is ready in under two minutes with a card number, expiry date, and CVV.

### Step 2: Fund your card with crypto

Choose your preferred cryptocurrency and network:

- **USDT (TRC-20)** — Best for most users. Transfer fee is about $1, confirmation in under 5 minutes on TRON
- **ETH (ERC-20)** — Works well for larger amounts. Fees vary with Ethereum network congestion
- **BNB (BEP-20)** — Lowest fees on Binance Smart Chain, usually under $0.50

For a new game release at $59.99, deposit $65-70 to cover the price plus any service fees and a small buffer.

### Step 3: Purchase on Steam

**For direct game purchase:**

1. Open Steam and find the game you want
2. Click **"Add to Cart"** then **"Purchase for myself"**
3. Select **"Visa"** or **"Mastercard"** as the payment method
4. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
5. Fill in the billing address (any valid address works)
6. Complete the purchase

**For Steam Wallet top-up:**

1. Go to your Steam account and click your username > **"View my wallet"**
2. Click **"Add funds to your Steam Wallet"**
3. Choose an amount ($5, $10, $25, $50, or $100)
4. Select **"Visa/Mastercard"** and enter your uCards card details
5. Complete the transaction

### Step 4: Download and play

Once the purchase processes, the game appears in your Steam library. Download and start playing.

## Steam + Crypto: Common Questions

**Does Steam accept Bitcoin?**
No. Steam removed Bitcoin support in 2017 due to price volatility and high fees. However, a virtual card funded with crypto works because Steam sees a standard Visa transaction.

**Can I refund a Steam game bought with crypto?**
Yes. Steam's standard refund policy applies regardless of payment method. If you request a refund within 14 days and have played under 2 hours, Steam refunds the amount back to your virtual card.

**What if my card gets declined on Steam?**
Make sure your uCards balance covers the purchase amount plus any currency conversion margin. If the issue persists, try topping up your Steam Wallet first, then purchasing from wallet balance.

**Can I buy Steam gift cards with crypto?**
Instead of gift cards, you can send Steam Wallet funds or buy games as gifts directly using your virtual card. This is simpler and avoids the markup that third-party gift card sellers charge.

**Are there limits on how much I can spend?**
Spending limits depend on your uCards card tier and balance. For large purchases, ensure your card has sufficient funds loaded. You can top up multiple times as needed.
