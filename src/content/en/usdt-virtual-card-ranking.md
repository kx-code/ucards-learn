## Common Issues and Solutions

### What to do if your top-up hasn't arrived?

1. Check if the transfer address is correct
2. Confirm the transaction status on a blockchain explorer
3. Verify you used the correct network (TRC20 addresses cannot be used for ERC20)
4. Contact uCards customer support with the transaction hash

### What to do if a transaction is declined?

1. Confirm your card has sufficient balance
2. Check if the merchant supports the card network (Visa/Mastercard)
3. Verify the transaction amount does not exceed the per-transaction limit
4. Try switching browsers or clearing your cache
