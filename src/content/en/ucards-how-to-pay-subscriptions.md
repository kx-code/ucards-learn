### Best Practices for Recurring Payments

1. **Fund monthly, not per-service** — Add enough for all subscriptions at once to minimize transaction fees
2. **Add a buffer** — Fund 10-15% above your total monthly subscription cost
3. **Set reminders** — Add calendar alerts 2 days before each billing date to ensure your card is funded
4. **Check balance regularly** — Log in to your uCards dashboard to verify your balance covers upcoming charges
5. **Keep your card active** — Regular use helps prevent any fraud detection flags that could block your card

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Payment Declined

If your card is declined during a subscription payment:

1. **Check your balance** — Ensure you have enough funds including a buffer
2. **Verify card details** — Make sure the card number, expiration, and CVV are correct
3. **Try again** — Some declines are temporary due to network timeouts
4. **Check the billing address** — Use an address that matches the region of the service (a US address works for most services)
5. **Contact support** — If the issue persists, reach out to uCards support

### Card Not Accepted

Very rarely, a merchant may not accept your card type. This is uncommon with Visa cards but can happen if:

- The merchant specifically blocks prepaid cards (rare)
- The merchant requires 3D Secure authentication that is not supported
- The merchant's fraud system flags the transaction

**Solution:** Try the payment again after 24 hours, or use a different card type if available on uCards.

### Subscription Renewal Failed

If a subscription fails to renew:

1. Check that your uCards balance covers the renewal amount
2. Log in to the subscription service and manually retry the payment
3. Update your card details if you received a new card

## Subscription Payment FAQ

### Can I use uCards for annual subscriptions?

Yes. Annual subscriptions work the same as monthly ones. Just ensure your card balance covers the full annual amount. Services like Amazon Prime, Adobe Creative Cloud annual plans, and domain name renewals all work.

### What happens if my balance is too low when a subscription renews?

The payment will fail. Most services will retry the payment over the next few days and send you an email notification. Fund your card promptly to avoid service interruption.

### Can I have multiple subscriptions on one uCards card?

Yes. You can use the same uCards card for as many subscriptions as you want, as long as your balance covers the total.

### Do free trials work with uCards?

Yes. Free trials that require a card on file work with uCards. The card will not be charged until the trial period ends. Just remember to fund your card before the trial converts to a paid subscription.

### Can I cancel subscriptions paid with uCards?

Yes. Cancel through the service's website or app as you normally would. You can also let your uCards balance go to zero, which will naturally prevent future charges — though this is not recommended if you have active subscriptions you want to keep.
