3. **Mobile money to crypto** — Services in Africa and Southeast Asia convert mobile money balances to USDT
4. **Crypto earning** — Freelance platforms like LaborX and CoinGigs pay in crypto directly

### Optimizing Your uCards Experience

- **Use USDT on TRON for funding** — It has the lowest fees and fastest confirmation times globally
- **Fund in batches** — Instead of frequent small deposits, fund your card with enough for a month of spending to minimize transaction fees
- **Check exchange rates** — Compare rates on your local exchange before buying USDT to get the best deal
- **Keep your wallet secure** — Use a hardware wallet or a reputable mobile wallet with biometric security

### Handling Currency Conversion

uCards cards are denominated in USD. When you make a purchase in a different currency, Visa handles the currency conversion automatically. Here is what to know:

- Visa uses competitive exchange rates, typically close to the mid-market rate
- Some merchants may charge a small currency conversion fee — this is set by the merchant, not uCards
- For the most predictable spending, use your card at merchants that charge in USD when possible

## Regional Limitations to Be Aware Of

While uCards works globally for online payments, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. **Online only** — uCards virtual cards are designed for online transactions. They cannot be used at physical point-of-sale terminals.
2. **Merchant restrictions** — A small number of merchants may block prepaid cards. This is rare but can happen with certain luxury retailers or high-risk merchants.
3. **Local regulations** — While uCards does not impose geographic restrictions, your country may have regulations about using cryptocurrency. Always comply with local laws.
4. **Internet access required** — You need internet access to manage your card and view card details.
