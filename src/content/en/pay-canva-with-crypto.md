## How to Pay Canva Pro with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Create your uCards virtual card

Go to [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk) and sign up. No identity documents required, no waiting period. Your virtual Visa card is created instantly with a full card number, expiry date, and CVV.

### Step 2: Deposit cryptocurrency

Fund your card with your preferred crypto:

- **USDT (TRC-20)** — Best option for most users. Transfer fee around $1, confirmed in about 3 minutes on TRON
- **ETH (ERC-20)** — Works well for larger deposits. Fees vary with network activity
- **BNB (BEP-20)** — Lowest fees on BSC, usually under $0.50

For monthly Canva Pro ($14.99), deposit $20 to cover the subscription plus fees and a buffer. For the annual plan ($119.99), deposit $130-135.

### Step 3: Subscribe to Canva Pro

1. Log in to your Canva account at [canva.com](https://canva.com)
2. Click the **gear icon** or go to **Settings** > **Billing & Plans**
3. Click **"Upgrade to Pro"** or **"Try Canva Pro"**
4. Select your plan: monthly ($14.99) or annual ($119.99)
5. Choose **"Credit or debit card"** as payment method
6. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
7. Fill in the billing address (any valid address works)
8. Click **"Start my free trial"** or **"Subscribe"** to confirm

Canva offers a 30-day free trial for new Pro subscribers. After the trial ends, your card is charged automatically.

### Step 4: Start designing

Your Canva Pro features are immediately available. Access premium templates, use the background remover, set up your Brand Kit, and take advantage of all Pro tools.

### What happens if payment fails?

If your card has insufficient balance when Canva tries to charge it:

1. Canva sends an email notification
2. Your account temporarily downgrades to the Free plan
3. Your designs and files remain accessible but Pro features are disabled
4. Once you top up and payment processes, Pro features are restored immediately

No designs or files are deleted during a downgrade.

## Canva + Crypto: Common Questions

**Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?**
Yes. Go to Canva Settings > Billing & Plans and switch to annual billing. Canva will charge your card for the full year and adjust your next renewal date.

**Does the 30-day free trial work with a virtual card?**
Yes. Canva starts the free trial by placing a temporary $1 authorization hold on your card. This is released within a few days. After 30 days, the full subscription amount is charged.

**Can I get a refund if I cancel Canva Pro?**
Canva offers a money-back guarantee within the first 30 days of a paid subscription. After that, you can cancel anytime and your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

**Will Canva know I am paying with crypto?**
No. Canva processes a standard Visa card transaction. The crypto funding is invisible to Canva.
