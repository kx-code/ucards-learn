## Thailand Regulation FAQ

### Is cryptocurrency legal in Thailand?

Yes, completely legal. Thailand enacted the "Digital Asset Act" in 2018, legally recognizing cryptocurrency trading. Exchanges operate under the supervision of SEC Thailand, making it one of the countries with the clearest regulatory framework in Southeast Asia.

### How are cryptocurrency taxes handled in Thailand?

In Thailand, profits from buying and selling cryptocurrency are taxed as capital gains (15% withholding tax). However, simply holding USDT for card top-up purposes does not trigger a taxable event.
