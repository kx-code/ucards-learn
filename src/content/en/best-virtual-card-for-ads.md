## Common Issues and Solutions

### Facebook Ads Billing Failed

**Possible causes**:

- Insufficient card balance
- Facebook risk control system flagged the transaction
- Billing address mismatch

**Solutions**:

- Top up immediately so your card balance exceeds the pending ad charges
- Use a Visa card (Facebook has a higher approval rate for Visa)
- Ensure the billing address matches your Facebook account information

### Google Ads Pre-Authorization Failed

Google initiates a pre-authorization verification when you first link a card. Make sure the card has at least $2 in balance. If it fails, wait 24 hours and retry, or switch to a Mastercard card type.

### TikTok Ads Card Linking Rejected

TikTok is stricter about billing address review. We recommend:

- Use a complete address in English
- Include street, city, state/province, and zip code
- Do not use obviously fake addresses

### Ad Account Suspended — Unrelated to Payment

If your ad account is suspended, it's usually due to ad content policy violations, not the payment method. Once you resolve the content compliance issue, the same uCards card can continue to be used.
