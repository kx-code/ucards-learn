## Why Are You Looking for a PST.net Alternative?

PST.net is a platform that provides virtual card services, and many users use it to pay for online subscriptions. But if you're searching for "PST.net alternative" or "PST.net review," you may have encountered the following issues:

- **Opaque fees** — Issuance fees, monthly fees, and top-up fees stacking up to actual costs far beyond expectations
- **KYC requirements** — Some operations require identity documents, contradicting the original promise of virtual card "convenience"
- **Unstable subscription payments** — Cards being declined or renewal failures at certain subscription services
- **Limited top-up methods** — Doesn't support your preferred top-up channel
- **Slow customer support** — Delayed responses when encountering issues
- **Card BIN flagged** — Certain subscription platforms may have already flagged PST.net's card BINs, causing payment failures

For users who need stable subscription payments, these issues directly affect your work and daily life.
