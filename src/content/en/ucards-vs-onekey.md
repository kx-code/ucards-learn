### Can OneKey hardware wallets top up uCards directly?

Yes. OneKey hardware wallets support TRON, ETH, and BSC networks. Simply select the corresponding network in your OneKey wallet and send USDT to uCards' top-up address. The process is no different from using any other wallet. We recommend using the TRC20 network for the lowest fees.

### Which cryptocurrencies does uCards support for top-ups?

uCards currently supports USDT (Tether) top-ups. USDT is the largest stablecoin by circulation, with 1 USDT approximately equal to 1 USD. USDT has sufficient liquidity on all three supported chains — TRON, ETH, and BSC. If you hold other cryptocurrencies (like BTC or ETH), you can first exchange them for USDT on an exchange, then top up uCards.

### Can uCards cards be used globally?

Yes. uCards' Visa, Mastercard, and Discover virtual cards can be used at online merchants worldwide, including major regions like the US, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. Specific availability depends on the merchant's card network support, not regional restrictions.

### What should I be aware of when migrating from OneKey Card?

Three main things to note: 1) Spend or withdraw your OneKey Card balance first to avoid scattered funds; 2) Update your payment methods on platforms (like ChatGPT, Netflix) promptly; 3) Keep your OneKey Card account open for a while to confirm there are no missed subscription charges.

### Does uCards have a referral reward program?

uCards has an invitation commission mechanism — you can earn commission rewards by inviting friends to register and use the platform. The specific commission rates and rules can be viewed within the uCards platform. Compared to OneKey Card, uCards' referral mechanism is more transparent and timely.

### What should I do if a payment fails?

uCards offers three card networks. If a payment is declined on one card, you can try using a card from a different network. For example, if Visa is declined, try Mastercard or Discover. Also make sure the card has sufficient balance and the billing address is filled in correctly. uCards' online support can help troubleshoot payment issues.
