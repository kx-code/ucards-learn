## FAQ

### What's the difference between a Visa virtual card and a physical Visa card?

A Visa virtual card has no physical plastic card — it only has a 16-digit card number, expiry date, and CVV, specifically designed for online payments. Virtual cards are issued extremely fast (uCards takes just 2-5 minutes), cost less (no card manufacturing or shipping fees), and carry no risk of loss or theft. The online spending experience is identical to a physical card.

### Can Visa virtual cards be used at ATMs?

Most Visa virtual cards don't support ATM withdrawals because there's no physical card. uCards Visa virtual cards are primarily designed for online spending. If you need ATM withdrawal functionality, consider digital banks like Revolut that offer physical cards.

### Can I use multiple Visa virtual cards at the same time?

### How is Visa virtual card security ensured?

uCards Visa virtual cards employ multiple layers of security: Visa Zero Liability policy protects against unauthorized transaction losses, TLS 1.3 encrypted communication, cards can be frozen at any time, and no personal identity information is collected (no KYC), eliminating data breach risk at the source.
