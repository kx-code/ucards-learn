- **Not available everywhere** — Global coverage is incomplete; users in some regions cannot register or access all features

Dupay supports cryptocurrency top-ups and offers virtual card services. However, some features may require KYC, the fee structure is not entirely transparent, and costs can be high.

- **Best for**: Users who need crypto top-ups
- **Not ideal for**: Users seeking maximum privacy and the lowest costs

### #4 RedotPay — Crypto Spending Platform

RedotPay focuses on cryptocurrency spending scenarios and supports multiple crypto top-ups. However, there may be monthly fees and KYC requirements.

- **Best for**: Everyday crypto spending
- **Not ideal for**: Dedicated ad spending, AI subscription use cases

### #5 Pyypl — Middle East Market Option

Pyypl has good coverage in the Middle East and supports fiat top-ups. However, it requires phone number verification, has limited crypto support, and fewer card types.

- **Best for**: Middle East users
- **Not ideal for**: Global users, crypto users
