## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the safest virtual credit card?

Safety depends on the provider and how you use it. Look for platforms with encrypted card delivery, two-factor authentication, and the ability to instantly freeze or cancel cards. Crypto-funded cards like uCards add an extra layer of safety since you only load what you need and no personal banking information is linked to the card.

### Can I use a virtual card for online subscriptions?

Yes, virtual cards work perfectly for online subscriptions including Netflix, Spotify, ChatGPT Plus, Amazon Prime, cloud hosting, and more. The card is charged automatically each billing cycle as long as there is sufficient balance.

### Do virtual cards work internationally?

Most virtual cards work internationally at any merchant that accepts the card network (Visa, Mastercard, or Discover). Crypto-funded virtual cards are especially useful for international payments because they bypass traditional banking restrictions and currency conversion fees.

### Is there a virtual card with no monthly fee?

Yes, several providers including uCards offer virtual cards without monthly maintenance fees. You typically pay only when you fund the card or make transactions. Always check the fee schedule before committing.

### Can I get a virtual card without KYC?

Yes. uCards provides fully functional virtual Visa and Mastercard cards without requiring any identity verification. You sign up with an email or crypto wallet, fund with USDT, and receive your card details instantly. This makes it one of the fastest and most private virtual card options available.
