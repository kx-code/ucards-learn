## How to Shop Online with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Create your uCards virtual Visa

Go to [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk) and sign up. The process takes under 2 minutes. No identity documents, no credit check, no KYC. Your virtual card is ready immediately.

### Step 2: Add funds with cryptocurrency

Choose your crypto and send it to your uCards deposit address:

- **USDT on TRON (TRC-20)** — Best for most users. Low fees, fast confirmation
- **USDT on Ethereum (ERC-20)** — Widely available
- **ETH** — Direct Ethereum transfer
- **BNB on BSC** — Low-cost option

Funds appear on your card within minutes. For USDT, the amount you send equals your card balance (minus a small fee).

### Step 3: Shop at any online store

Go to any website that accepts Visa. At checkout:

1. Select **"Credit/Debit Card"** as your payment method
2. Enter your uCards card number, expiry, and CVV
3. Provide a billing address (you can use any valid address)
4. Confirm the purchase

The charge is deducted from your card balance, and your order is processed normally.

### Step 4: Track your spending

Monitor all transactions in your uCards dashboard. See exactly where your crypto is being spent, with real-time balance updates.

## Crypto Shopping Tips

### Use USDT for predictable spending

USDT is pegged 1:1 to USD. When you add $100 USDT to your card, you have $100 to spend (minus fees). This predictability makes budgeting easy.

### Keep a buffer on your card

Always maintain a small buffer ($5-10) above your expected purchase amount. This covers currency conversion fees and prevents declined transactions.

### Check supported networks before sending

Make sure you're sending crypto on the correct network. TRC-20 USDT and ERC-20 USDT are different — sending on the wrong network will result in lost funds.

### Compare network fees

| Network | USDT Transfer Fee | Confirmation Time |

TRC-20 is the most cost-effective for most transactions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use uCards for physical stores?**
uCards virtual cards are designed for online transactions. They don't work at physical point-of-sale terminals.

**What happens if a purchase is refunded?**
Refunds are credited back to your uCards card balance. Processing time depends on the merchant, typically 5-10 business days.

**Is there a maximum transaction amount?**
Transaction limits depend on your card's balance. You can spend up to your available balance on any single purchase.

**Can I use uCards for recurring payments?**
Yes. Your virtual card works for subscriptions and recurring billing, including monthly services like Netflix and ChatGPT Plus.
