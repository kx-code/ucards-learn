But paying for TikTok Ads can be problematic for crypto-native businesses and international advertisers. A virtual card funded by cryptocurrency solves these payment challenges.

- **Card verification issues** — TikTok's payment system can be strict about card validation

### Step 3: Add your card to TikTok Ads Manager

1. Log in to [ads.tiktok.com](https://ads.tiktok.com)
2. Go to **Settings** > **Payment** > **Payment Methods**
3. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
4. Select **"Credit Card"** or **"Debit Card"**
5. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
6. Set the billing address (use a valid address in a supported country)
7. Click **"Save"** or **"Add Card"**

TikTok may place a temporary authorization hold to verify the card.

### Step 4: Create your campaigns

Set up your ad campaigns, define your target audience, and launch. TikTok will charge your card based on your campaign spend.

## TikTok Ads Billing Explained

### Manual vs automatic payments

| Payment Mode            | How It Works                     | Best For                    |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Automatic (recommended) | Charged when you hit a threshold | Ongoing campaigns           |

For automatic payments, TikTok charges your card when your spend reaches a threshold (typically starting at $20, increasing over time to $50, $100, $200, etc.).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does TikTok accept crypto directly?**
No. TikTok Ads accepts Visa and Mastercard. uCards converts your crypto into a Visa payment.

**Can I target any country with TikTok Ads?**
Yes, as long as the country is in TikTok's supported targeting list. Your payment method doesn't affect targeting options.

**What if my card gets rejected by TikTok?**
Ensure your card has sufficient balance and the billing address is in a supported country. Try again after topping up.

**Can I run TikTok Shop ads?**
Yes. TikTok Shop advertising uses the same payment system and works with your uCards card.
