## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use the same card for multiple AI tools?**
Yes. Your uCards virtual card can be used for as many subscriptions as you want, as long as the balance is sufficient.

**What if a subscription charge fails?**
Top up your card and the service will retry the charge. Most AI tools retry failed payments within 1-3 days.

**Is USDT better than ETH for top-ups?**
USDT is generally better because it's pegged to USD. You always know exactly how much you're adding. ETH value fluctuates, which can lead to unexpected shortfalls.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Simply cancel the subscription on the AI tool's website. Your uCards card will stop being charged automatically.
