## How to Pay AliExpress with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Create a uCards virtual card

Go to [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk) and sign up for free. No KYC, no documents. Your virtual Visa card is ready immediately.

### Step 2: Fund your card with crypto

Deposit cryptocurrency to your uCards account:

- **USDT on TRON (TRC-20)** — Best option for AliExpress shopping. Low fees (~$1), fast confirmation (3 min)
- **USDT on Ethereum (ERC-20)** — Alternative if you use Ethereum
- **ETH** — Direct Ethereum transfer
- **BNB on BSC** — Binance Smart Chain, ultra-low fees

For AliExpress shopping, we recommend topping up the estimated cost of your order plus 10% to cover currency conversion and shipping.

### Step 3: Add your card to AliExpress

1. Log in to your AliExpress account
2. Go to **"My AliExpress"** > **"Payment Methods"**
3. Click **"Add a new card"**
4. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
5. Set the billing address
6. Save the card

### Step 4: Place your order

1. Browse AliExpress and add items to your cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Select your uCards Visa card as the payment method
4. Confirm the order

AliExpress will process the payment immediately. You'll see the charge on your uCards dashboard.

## AliExpress Shopping Tips for Crypto Users

### Calculate total costs before topping up

AliExpress prices don't always include shipping. Check the total cost including shipping before loading your card.

### Use USDT for accurate budgeting

Since USDT is pegged to USD, you know exactly how much you're spending. If you use ETH or BNB, price fluctuations between top-up and purchase could affect your available balance.

### Allow time for shipping

AliExpress orders typically take 2-4 weeks to arrive. Your uCards transaction is charged when the order is placed, not when it ships.

### Handle disputes through AliExpress

If you have an issue with an order, use AliExpress's built-in dispute system. Refunds are credited back to your uCards card within 5-10 business days.
