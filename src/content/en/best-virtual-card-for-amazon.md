## Common Issues and Solutions

### Payment Declined

**Troubleshooting**:

- Is the card balance sufficient (including shipping and taxes)?
- Does the billing address match the Amazon site region?
- Is the card network supported by the merchant?

**Solutions**:

- Top up to exceed the total order amount (including shipping and tax)
- If Visa is declined, switch to a Mastercard card type
- Purchase an Amazon Gift Card as an alternative

### Pre-Authorization Holds

Amazon may initiate a pre-authorization hold (freezing the amount) when you place an order, with the actual charge applied after shipping. Make sure your card balance is $1-2 more than the order amount to pass the pre-authorization verification.

### Cross-Site Shopping

If your Amazon account is registered in one region but you want to shop on another region's site, uCards virtual cards are not region-locked and can be used on any Amazon site. Just make sure the billing address format is correct.
