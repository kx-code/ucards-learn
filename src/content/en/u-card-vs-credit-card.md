## Core Concepts

### What Is a U Card?

A U Card is a virtual prepaid credit card offered by the uCards platform. Funded with USDT cryptocurrency, it requires no KYC identity verification and works at millions of online merchants worldwide. The core mechanism is **fund first, spend later** — you can only spend what you have already loaded onto the card.

### What Is a Credit Card?

A credit card is a revolving credit instrument issued by a bank or financial institution. Cardholders can borrow against a credit limit to make purchases and receive a monthly statement for repayment. Any unpaid balance accrues interest at a high rate.

### The Difference in One Sentence

> **A U Card lets you spend your own money (prepaid). A credit card lets you spend the bank's money (borrowed).**
