## How to Pay Shopify with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Create your uCards virtual card

Sign up at [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk). No KYC, no credit check, no bank account needed. Your virtual Visa card is created in under 2 minutes.

### Step 2: Fund your card with crypto

Deposit cryptocurrency to your uCards account:

- **USDT on TRON (TRC-20)** — Best option. $1 fee, 3-minute confirmation
- **USDT on Ethereum (ERC-20)** — Alternative network
- **ETH** — Direct Ethereum payment
- **BNB on BSC** — Ultra-low fee option

For Shopify, we recommend loading at least 2 months of subscription fees at once to avoid service interruptions.

**Recommended top-up amounts:**

| Shopify Plan | Monthly Cost | Top-Up (2 months) |
| ------------ | ------------ | ----------------- |
| Basic        | $39          | $85               |
| Shopify      | $105         | $225              |
| Advanced     | $399         | $840              |
| Starter      | $5           | $15               |

### Step 3: Add your card to Shopify

1. Log in to your Shopify admin panel
2. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
3. Click **"Add payment method"**
4. Enter your uCards card details (number, expiry, CVV)
5. Set the billing address
6. Save the payment method

### Step 4: Your subscription is active

Shopify will charge your card monthly. The first charge happens immediately when you select a plan.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I pay for Shopify apps too?**
Yes. Shopify app subscriptions are billed through Shopify and charged to your card automatically.

**What if my Shopify subscription fails?**
Shopify gives a grace period. Top up your card and Shopify will retry the charge. Your store stays active during the grace period.

**Can I use uCards for Shopify trial?**
Yes. Shopify requires a card for the free trial. Your uCards card works for this purpose. You won't be charged until the trial ends.

**Does this work for Shopify POS?**
Yes. Shopify POS subscription is billed the same way and works with your uCards card.
