## FAQ

### How important is PST.net's BIN optimization?

BIN (Bank Identification Number) determines the card's issuing bank and regional information. Some ad platforms apply stricter or more lenient risk control to specific BINs. PST.net provides verified BINs that can improve ad payment success rates. But for most users, uCards' three card network options already provide sufficient flexibility. Unless you're a professional ad buyer, the marginal value of BIN optimization is limited.

### Can uCards be used for multi-account ad buying?

uCards' zero KYC means you can open multiple cards for different ad accounts. This is very useful in multi-account ad buying scenarios. But please note that you must comply with each ad platform's terms of service — multi-account operations may violate platform rules. uCards provides multiple card networks and card types to help you manage multiple ad accounts within compliant boundaries.

### What does PST.net's team management feature do?

PST.net's team management feature allows you to assign different permissions and spending limits to team members. For example, you can let team members use virtual cards for ad spending while limiting individual transaction amounts. This is valuable for ad agencies. uCards is currently more suited for individual users — if your team is small, you can achieve similar effects through multi-card management.

### Is it worth switching from PST.net to uCards?

If you're an individual user or small advertiser, it's worth switching. Based on our cost calculations, you can save $200-500+ annually. Switching steps: 1) Register and open a card on uCards; 2) Test payment success rates with small ad spend; 3) Gradually migrate once satisfied. We recommend keeping your PST.net account for a while to ensure no missed ad charges.

### Is uCards' Discover card good for ad payments?

Discover cards have decent acceptance on ad platforms in the US market. If Visa or Mastercard encounter payment issues, Discover is a worthwhile alternative to try. uCards' unique advantage of offering three card networks means you can quickly switch between Visa, Mastercard, and Discover to find the card that works best for your ad accounts.

### Which platform has higher top-up limits?

Specific limits vary by card type and account status. PST.net may require KYC for large top-ups. uCards has no KYC restrictions, and top-up limits depend on card type. If you need to make large top-ups, we recommend contacting uCards support to learn about specific limit policies.
