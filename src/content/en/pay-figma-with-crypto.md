## Why Pay Figma with Cryptocurrency?

Figma has become the industry standard for UI/UX design, used by companies like Airbnb, Uber, Microsoft, and thousands of design agencies worldwide. Its real-time collaboration features, prototyping tools, and developer handoff capabilities make it indispensable for modern product teams.

But Figma's paid plans can be challenging to subscribe to without a traditional credit card. Freelance designers, distributed teams, and agencies in regions with limited banking infrastructure often struggle with payment. A virtual card funded with cryptocurrency provides a clean solution: a Visa card number that Figma accepts, no KYC verification, and no bank account needed.

Key differences between plans:

- **Professional**: Unlimited projects, team libraries, advanced prototyping, sharing permissions
- **Organization**: Everything in Pro plus design system analytics, centralized admin, branch merging, private plugins
- **Enterprise**: Everything in Org plus SSO, dedicated support, custom security policies, design ops tools

For most freelance designers, the Professional plan at $15/editor/month provides everything needed. For a 5-person team, that is $75/month.

## How to Pay Figma with Crypto: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Get your uCards virtual card

Visit [ucards.uk](https://ucards.uk) and create an account. No documents, no KYC, no waiting. Your virtual Visa card number, expiry date, and CVV are generated instantly.

### Step 2: Deposit cryptocurrency

Load your card using your preferred crypto:

- **USDT (TRC-20)** — Recommended. Transfer fee around $1, confirmed in roughly 3 minutes on the TRON network
- **ETH (ERC-20)** — Good for larger deposits. Fees vary based on Ethereum network activity
- **BNB (BEP-20)** — Lowest fees on BSC, typically under $0.50

For a single Figma Professional seat at $15/month, deposit $25 to cover the subscription plus a buffer. For a 5-editor team at $75/month, deposit $90.

### Step 3: Add your card to Figma

1. Log in to [figma.com](https://figma.com) and click your avatar
2. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
3. Click **"Edit"** next to Payment method
4. Select **"Credit or debit card"**
5. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
6. Fill in the billing address (any valid address works)
7. Click **"Save"** to confirm

Figma will verify the card with a small temporary authorization hold. This is standard and the hold is released within a few business days.

### Step 4: Upgrade or create your team

After saving your card:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Plans**
2. Select **"Professional"** or **"Organization"**
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Confirm the number of editor seats
5. Click **"Upgrade"** to activate

Your team now has access to all paid features immediately.

## Keeping Your Figma Subscription Active

### Monthly billing management

Figma charges per editor seat on a monthly cycle. When you add new editors, the cost increases proportionally for the remainder of the billing period.

### What if payment fails?

If your card balance is insufficient:

1. Figma sends a billing alert email to all organization admins
2. The team retains access for a grace period
3. After the grace period, the team downgrades to the Free plan
4. Files and projects are preserved but advanced features are disabled
5. Top up your card and Figma will process the payment on the next retry

No design files are deleted during a downgrade. However, team libraries and shared components may become read-only until the subscription is restored.

## Figma Organization vs Professional: Which to Choose

Choose **Professional** if you are a freelancer, independent designer, or small team (1-5 people) who needs unlimited projects and team libraries.

Choose **Organization** if you manage a design team that needs centralized admin controls, design system analytics, branching and merging, and private plugins. The Organization plan is designed for teams of 5 or more editors who need governance over their design system.

## Figma + Crypto: Common Questions

**Can I switch from Professional to Organization later?**
Yes. Upgrade your plan at any time in Figma settings. The price difference is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

**Can I add editor seats mid-month?**
Yes. Figma charges a prorated amount for new editors added during the billing period. The additional charge is applied to your virtual card immediately.

**What if I remove an editor seat?**
The seat is removed immediately, and the cost reduction takes effect on your next billing date. No partial refund is issued for the current period.

**Will my team see my payment details?**
No. Only organization admins can see billing information. Team members cannot view your card number or payment method.
