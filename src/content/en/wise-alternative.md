## Why Wise Is No Longer the Only Option

Wise (formerly TransferWise) is renowned for low-cost international transfers, with over 16 million users. But if you are searching for "Wise alternative" or "Wise fees," you may have noticed:

- **No cryptocurrency top-up support** — USDT holders must first convert through an exchange, adding extra fees and time
- **Strict KYC verification** — Registration requires ID documents and proof of address; some applicants are rejected
- **Slow card delivery** — Physical cards take 2–4 weeks for international shipping; virtual cards have limited features
- **Stacking fees** — While individual transfer fees are low, the combined cost of exchange + top-up + monthly fees adds up
- **Not truly global** — Not all countries have access to all features; some regions face severe restrictions
- **Not ideal as a spending card** — Wise is better for transfers; its spending features and experience lag behind dedicated virtual card platforms

If you hold cryptocurrency or value privacy, these limitations are even more apparent. **uCards** and other alternatives offer better solutions.
