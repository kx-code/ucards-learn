## Beginner's Introduction: What Is a Virtual Credit Card?

If you have never used a virtual credit card, do not worry — this guide starts from the absolute basics and walks you through the entire process from registration to your first purchase.

### Virtual Credit Card = Digital Credit Card

Simply put, a virtual credit card is a set of bank card details (card number, expiration date, CVV) that exists in digital form and is used for online payments. It functions exactly the same as the credit card in your wallet, except:

- There is no plastic card
- You do not need to visit a bank to apply
- It is delivered instantly and ready to use immediately
- Usually no ID documents are required

### 5 Core Concepts Beginners Need to Know

| Concept           | Explanation                                               | Analogy                              |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **USDT**          | A cryptocurrency pegged 1:1 to the US dollar (stablecoin) | Digital dollars                      |
| **TRC20**         | The USDT transfer protocol on the TRON network            | Like a bank account number format    |
| **Top-up**        | Transferring USDT into your virtual card platform         | Like loading a transit card          |
| **Card creation** | Receiving a set of card number/CVV/expiration date        | Like opening a new bank card         |
| **CVV**           | The 3-digit security code on the back of a card           | Password verification during payment |
