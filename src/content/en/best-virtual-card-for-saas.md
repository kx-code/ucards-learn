## Common Issues and Solutions

### SaaS Billing Failed

**Causes**:

- Insufficient card balance
- SaaS platform updated its payment system
- Pre-authorization verification failed

**Solutions**:

- Top up immediately (TRC-20 USDT arrives in 3 minutes)
- Re-enter the card information

### Multiple SaaS Services Billing on the Same Day

If multiple SaaS subscriptions renew on the same day, make sure your card balance covers the total of all charges. If funds are tight, contact each SaaS provider's support to adjust renewal dates.
