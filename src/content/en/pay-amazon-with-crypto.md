## Why Buy on Amazon with Cryptocurrency?

Amazon is the world's largest online retailer with over 600 million products. But Amazon doesn't accept cryptocurrency directly. No Bitcoin, no Ethereum, no USDT — only Visa, Mastercard, and a few other traditional methods.

Choose your preferred cryptocurrency and send it to your uCards deposit address:

- **BNB on BSC** — Ultra-low fees

For Amazon shopping, add enough to cover your purchase plus a 5% buffer for currency conversion.

### Step 3: Add your card to Amazon

1. Log in to your Amazon account
2. Go to **"Account"** > **"Your Payments"** > **"Add a payment method"**
3. Enter your uCards card number, expiry date, and CVV
4. Set the billing address (use the address associated with your Amazon account)
5. Save the card

### Step 4: Shop normally

Browse Amazon, add items to your cart, and checkout using your uCards Visa card. Amazon processes it like any other Visa payment.

### Step 5: Monitor your balance

Track your card balance in the uCards dashboard. If you're making multiple purchases, make sure your balance covers all of them.

## Tips for Shopping Amazon with a Virtual Card

### Keep a buffer for holds

Amazon sometimes places authorization holds (especially for digital purchases or pre-orders). Keep an extra $5-10 on your card to cover these temporary holds.

### Use the correct billing address

Amazon verifies the billing address against your card. Use the same address you entered when setting up the card on Amazon's website.

### Large purchases

For expensive items ($100+), make sure your card has enough balance including the buffer. Amazon will decline the transaction if the balance is insufficient.

### Returns and refunds

If you return an item, Amazon refunds to your uCards card. The refund appears in your card balance within 5-10 business days.
