## Common Issues Affecting Card Issuance Speed

### Why is my top-up confirmation slow?

Blockchain network confirmation times increase during congestion. Recommendations:

- Use TRC20 instead of ERC20 (faster confirmation)
- Increase gas fees slightly to speed up confirmation
- Avoid topping up during peak network hours

### Why does KYC review take so long?

KYC review time depends on several factors:

- Whether the submitted documents are clear
- Whether manual review is needed
- The length of the review queue
- The platform's review capacity
