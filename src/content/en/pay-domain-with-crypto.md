## Domain Registration Tips

### Enable auto-renewal

Domains expire annually. Enable auto-renewal at your registrar and keep your uCards card funded to avoid losing your domain.

### Set renewal reminders

Domain renewal typically costs $10-15/year for .com. Set a calendar reminder to top up your card before the renewal date.

### Use WHOIS privacy

Most registrars offer free WHOIS privacy. Enable it to keep your registration details private.

### Register for multiple years

Lock in current prices by registering for 2-5 years. This also prevents accidentally losing your domain due to missed renewals.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I buy crypto domains (.eth, .crypto) with uCards?**
Blockchain domains like .eth (ENS) are purchased with ETH on the Ethereum blockchain, not through traditional registrars. However, you can use uCards for traditional domains like .com, .net, .io.

**What happens if my domain auto-renewal fails?**
The registrar will send reminder emails. Most give a 30-day grace period to renew. Top up your card and manually renew, or the registrar will retry the charge.

**Can I transfer domains purchased with uCards?**
Yes. Your payment method doesn't affect domain transfers. You can transfer your domain to any registrar at any time.

**Is it anonymous?**
While your card payment is private, domain registrars may still collect contact information during registration. Use WHOIS privacy and consider the privacy laws in your jurisdiction.
